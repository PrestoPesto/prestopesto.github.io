# Effects

##### Bleeding

`bld`
Takes X damage each turn until cured

##### Distracted

`dst`
-30% accuracy for X turns

##### Corrupted

`cor`
Max health halved for X turns, does not re-heal on end

##### Regen

`reg`
Heal X health each turn, X reduced by 1 at start of turn, until X is 0

##### Stunned

`stn`
stn
Loses X turns
